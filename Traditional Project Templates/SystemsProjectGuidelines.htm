Systems Project Guidelines

  • Meet one hour per week with Faculty Advisor - record minutes and deliver to advisor

  • Meet with Client on a regular basis - keep them informed - record minutes and copy advisor

  • Prepare Time Line based on Course Outline

  •  use MS Project

  • compare Estimated to Actual

  • aim for completion of Phase II by week before study week (use study week to start coding)

  • aim for completion of Phase V by second week of April

  • public demonstration (Phase VI) takes place first week in May (everyone is expected to attend and demonstrate their project)

  •  Prepare Data Flow Diagrams

  • indicate all inputs, processes, and outputs

  • create a Data Dictionary

  •  Document as you go

  • Systems Documentation

  • User Documentation/Manual

  • Include all standards - Date formats, passwords (types and lengths), codes (customer numbers, account numbers), etc.

  • Example manuals of past winners are available in John Scott's office

  •  System should include

  • Log In Screens with passwords

  • Levels of Security

  • Back Up and Restore procedures

  • Help System

  • Audit Trail

  • History Files (Archive) 

  • At the end of Phase II the design must be completed, presented and approved by Faculty Advisor AND Client BEFORE Coding begins
